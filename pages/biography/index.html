Biography

Feb 22, 2020

I’m a Bioinformatics Post-doctoral scholar with Dr. Felix Feng at the University of California, San Francisco (UCSF). I’m stationed at the UCSF Helen Diller Family Comprehensive Cancer Center.

I did my PhD in Bioinformatics from the University of British Columbia (UBC) under the supervision of Dr. Colin Collins and Dr. Cenk Sahinalp. I worked in conjunction with Cancer Data Science Laboratory at the National Cancer Institite (NCI). After my PhD, I continued working as a Post-doctoral scholar with Dr. Collins at Laboratory for Advanced Genome Analysis (LAGA), Vancouver Prostate Center for a year.

I reveived my undergraduate training (B.Tech) in Biotechnology from Kathmandu University, Nepal (2009). After graduation, I spent a couple of years working as Resarch Associate at The Center for Molecular Dynamics Nepal (CMDN) before moving to Canada for my PhD.
