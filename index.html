Raunak Shrestha

Post-doctoral Research Scholar (Bioinformatics)

University of California, San Francisco (UCSF)

I’m a Bioinformatics Post-doctoral Research Scholar working with Dr. Felix Feng at the University of California, San Francisco (UCSF). I’m stationed at the UCSF Helen Diller Family Comprehensive Cancer Center. I’m also an Adjunct Research Scientist at Nepal Applied Mathematics & Informatics Institute (NAAMII) where I’m leading a Bioinformatics team.

My main interest lies in studying how complex patterns of high-throughput biology (-omics) data can be integrated and analyzed together to associate genotypes-to-phenotypes in clinical context. My research focuses on understanding molecular mechanisms of treatment-resistant prostate cancer through computational and statistical analysis of genomic data.
