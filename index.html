Raunak Shrestha

Assistant Professional Researcher (Computational Biology),

University of California, San Francisco (UCSF)

I am a Computational Biology Research Scientist with Dr. Felix Feng at UCSF. My primary research location is the UCSF HDFC Cancer Center. Additionally, I hold Adjunct Research Scientist position at the Nepal Applied Mathematics & Informatics Institute for Research (NAAMII), where I am leading the Computational Genomics Laboratory. [My Bio]

My main interest lies in understanding cancer progression and treatment resistance through the development and application of computational genomics approaches.

I am actively looking for academic faculty position.

Computational Genomics Lab at NAAMII

In Computational Genomics Lab at Nepal Applied Mathematics & Informatics Institute for Research (NAAMII), we aim to transform global health through the application of computational genomics. Our areas of focus includes genomics of some of the major communicable and non-communicable diseases prevalent in Nepal, with a particular emphasis on better understanding disease progression and treatment resistance.
