I’m a Bioinformatics Post-doctoral Research Scholar working with Dr. Felix Feng at the University of California, San Francisco (UCSF). I’m stationed at the UCSF Helen Diller Family Comprehensive Cancer Center.

My research focuses on understanding molecular mechanisms of treatment-resistant prostate cancer through computational and statistical analysis of genomic data. [ My Curriculum Vitae] [ My Biography]
