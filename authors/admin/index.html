Raunak Shrestha

Assistant Professor,

Center for Prostate Disease Research (CPDR)

I am an Assistant Professor (Computational Biology) at Center for Prostate Disease Research (CPDR) / Uniformed Services University (USU), Department of Surgery. [My Bio]

My main interest lies on elucidating the drivers of cancer progression and therapeutic resistance, with the goal of advancing personalized medicine and improving clinical outcomes for cancer patients by understanding the complex interplay of the genome, transcriptome, proteome, and epigenome through the development and application of computational genomics.
